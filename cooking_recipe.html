6 Cheese Mac n Cheese

How to make the best Mac n Cheese you'll ever have!

This makes 8-10 servings.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb Smoked Gouda
  • 1/2 lb Parmesan
  • 1/2 lb Colby Jack
  • 1/2 lb Provolone
  • 1/2 sharp white Cheddar
  • 1/2 sharp Cheddar
  • 1/2 mild Cheddar
  • 32 oz block of Velveeta
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 boxes of macaroni shells
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • Seasonings: Salt, Pepper, Paprika, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Italian Seasoning

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook macaroni noodles according to directions on package.
  3. Meanwhile, cut/shred all the cheese.
  4. Butter sides and bottom of baking dish.
  5. Drain noodles & pour noodles into baking dish.
  6. In a bowl, Crack open 2 eggs and mix together with 1 cup heavy cream, salt, paprika, parsley, and pepper.
  7. Pour over noodles and into baking dish and stir to mix well
  8. Pour 3/4 of the cheese into the baking dish and mix well with noodles
  9. Bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Take out and stir. Add more cheese and bread crumbs/seasonings on top.
  11. Put back into oven and bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  12. Wait for 10 minutes before serving and enjoy!
